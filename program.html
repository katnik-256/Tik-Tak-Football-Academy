Youth Development Program

Ages 5-12 | Building Fundamentals & Love for the Game

2-3 sessions per week
Small group training
Fun competitions

Our Youth Development Program focuses on introducing young players to soccer while developing fundamental skills in a fun, engaging environment. We emphasize technical development, coordination, and sportsmanship.

Learn More Schedule & Fees

Program Focus

  • Ball mastery and coordination
  • Basic technical skills (passing, dribbling, shooting)
  • Introduction to tactical concepts
  • Age-appropriate physical development
  • Social skills and teamwork

Benefits

  • Professional coaching from licensed trainers
  • Progressive curriculum tailored to age groups
  • Positive, encouraging environment
  • Focus on long-term player development
  • Regular progress assessments

Age Groups

U6-U8

5-7 years

Introduction to soccer through fun games and activities. Focus on basic motor skills and enjoyment.

  • 1-hour sessions
  • 6:1 player-coach ratio
  • Non-competitive environment

U9-U10

8-9 years

Developing technical skills and introducing basic tactics. Small-sided games for maximum participation.

  • 75-minute sessions
  • 8:1 player-coach ratio
  • Local friendlies

U11-U12

10-11 years

Advanced technical development and introduction to positional play. Preparing for competitive soccer.

  • 90-minute sessions
  • 10:1 player-coach ratio
  • Local tournaments

Competitive Teams Program

Ages 13-18 | League Play & Tournament Competition

3-4 sessions per week
League competition
Tournament travel

Our Competitive Teams Program offers high-level training and competition for dedicated players. Teams compete in regional leagues and tournaments with a focus on player development and team success.

Learn More Tryout Information

Program Focus

  • Advanced technical refinement
  • Position-specific training
  • Tactical understanding and game intelligence
  • Physical conditioning and athletic development
  • Mental preparation and leadership

Benefits

  • Professional coaching staff with competitive experience
  • Video analysis and performance reviews
  • College recruitment support
  • High-level competition environment
  • Comprehensive player development

Team Levels

Development Team

For players new to competitive soccer or developing their skills. Focus on technical development with introductory competitive experience.

  • Local league play
  • 2-3 tournaments per year
  • 4 training sessions/week

Premier Team

High-level competitive teams with regional and national tournament experience. For committed players with advanced skills.

  • Regional league play
  • 4-6 tournaments per year
  • 4 training sessions/week

Elite Team

Our highest level of competition for top players with aspirations of collegiate or professional soccer.

  • National league play
  • 6-8 tournaments per year
  • 5 training sessions/week
